About the Owner

Local Service. Built in Peterborough.

Born and raised in Peterborough, Ryan founded Paradis Property Maintenance in 2014 with a commitment to reliable service and quality work. Since then, the company has grown steadily year after year through hard work, consistency, and a strong reputation in the community. Ryan takes pride in serving local homeowners and businesses with dependable property maintenance they can trust.


We take care of residential, commercial, and cottage properties all year round—anything from regular upkeep to bigger cleanups. No matter the job, we always try to treat every property like it’s our own.
